Our Project's Purpose

CNI (Conserving Nature Initiative) is a tree planting movement that encourages people to conserve the earth by planting trees located around Visayas and Mindanao.

Mission

To safeguard environmental integrity, improve its quality, and promote economic viability in the interests of public health.

Vision

To create a world that is able to protect its natural resources, one committed to the pursuit of sustainable development, and one with a clean, healthy environment that raises the standard of living for its people today and in the future.

Planting the trees

Trees are an investment. How well that investment grows depends on several factors including, the type of tree planted, its location and the care provided. Getting your new tree off to a healthy start will help the tree mature to its full size and ensures it will provide environmental, economic, and social benefits throughout its lifetime.
